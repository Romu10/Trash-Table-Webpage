TurtleBot 4 | Our garbage handlers

We introduce our innovative solution for managing waste in cafeterias using robotics technology. Our project focuses on utilizing the TurtleBot 4 platform equipped with specialized features to efficiently handle waste disposal tasks. At the heart of our project is a system designed to locate and navigate to designated waste collection tables strategically placed within the cafeteria. These tables are intelligently designed with built-in receptacles for easy disposal of waste by cafeteria patrons. Using advanced localization and navigation algorithms, our TurtleBot 4 robot autonomously moves throughout the cafeteria, identifying and approaching the waste collection tables. Once at a table, the robot employs a sophisticated elevator mechanism to lift the waste bin, facilitating the efficient collection of waste deposited by cafeteria patrons.

Our webpage serves as a comprehensive resource to learn more about our project, including detailed descriptions of the technology utilized, the functionality of the TurtleBot 4 platform, and the benefits of our waste management solution in cafeteria environments.
